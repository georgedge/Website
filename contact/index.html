How to get in touch

A tip of the hat, a tap on the elbow, a nod… please get in touch to talk about our stock or to tell us about yours. Success in farming, especially breeding, is heavily dependent on our ability to network and share information. Supply and demand are always shifting as standards and trends are always changing. We’re interested in hearing your ideas and telling you about ours, we love talking about cattle and the industry and we look forward to getting to know you.

There are many ways to get in touch with us, take your pick and give as much information as you can so that we can get the right person with the right information back to as quickly as possible. At the moment the farm is closed to visitors but we look forward to welcoming you when COVID restrictions allow.
