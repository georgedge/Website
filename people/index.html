Meet The Bush Farm Family

Farming is a family pursuit at Bush Farm with a heritage of cattle breeding going back over a century. Nano and Pappi both come from families who have achieved distinctions at Show events throughout the country down through the years. Together they have built a renowned stock of Angus and Charolais animals who are sought after beef and dairy farmers alike. Children and grandchildren all play a roll on the farm and formed close bonds through farming.

Nano

Coming from a famous Angus family, Nano has a keen eye for quality animals and is well known for selecting young animals who later grow to be champions. Nano is the expert selector on the farm and manages the breeding program.

Papi

Born and reared on a farm, Papi has been handling cattle all his life and has huge passion for the animals. Papi is the boss on the farm, from the youngest child to the biggest bull, everyone takes their lead from Papi.

Laura

Laura has qualified under the Teagasc Green Programme and has extensive knowledge on Principles of Agriculture, Farm Safety, Soil Maintenance, Farm Performance and Business Planning. Laura is helping to modernise the farm merging the latest theory and practice with tried-and-true traditional practices

Ruthie

Ruthie has a culinary background and has created a business supplying meat from the farm to homes and restaurants in the area. Ruthie uses her knowledge of food to help promote the more traditional cuts of beef producing techniques and recipes to customers.
