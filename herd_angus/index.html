Explore Our Angus Stock

The Angus breed has a storied history of providing the best possible cattle genetics to farmers and ranchers worldwide. Since 1873, people have looked to Angus to fulfill genetic requirements — calving ease, high growth, carcass value — that set the stage for a profitable cow herd.

The proof is in the genetics. Angus cattle are known for traits that can make a real difference in cow herd profitability, including calving ease, marbling and growth. Farmers and ranchers who have raised Angus cattle for generations say the cattle are lowmaintenance, adaptable and that their docile nature makes them unrivaled beef industry leaders.
